Three Things Every Plant Asks For

🌞

Light

Indirect sunlight is the sweetest for indoor greenery. Keep plants near bright, filtered windows and avoid harsh midday beams.

💧

Water

Water gently and regularly, allowing soil to dry slightly between sessions. A steady rhythm keeps roots happy without overwatering.

🌡

Warmth

Keep plants cozy in a warm corner. Most houseplants love a stable 18–24°C with a touch of humidity for fresh, glowing growth.

Plant care guide

Plant Needs Care schedule Details
Snake Plant Low light, dry soil Water every 2–3 weeks Let the soil dry almost fully between waterings.
Monstera Bright indirect light Water once a week Keep humidity moderate and wipe large leaves often.
Pothos Medium light, moist soil Water every 7–10 days Trim trailing vines and avoid soggy soil.
Spider Plant Indirect light, well-drained soil Water every 10–14 days Fresh soil and bright shade keep the plant thriving.
